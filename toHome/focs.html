Report to Mission Control for Instructions!


Hello Specialists! Thank you for reporting promptly to receive your mission assignment. We will have a series of tasks to complete in order to work toward project completion goals. The itinerary for each week is listed below:

  • MONDAY → review assignment details | record mission statement | gather with team for collaboration
  • TUESDAY → research approaches to address problem | discuss ideas and brainstorm | record ideas
  • WEDNESDAY → decide on final design | create drawing, blueprint, or flowchart of solution | start prototype
  • THURSDAY → complete prototype | test prototype and fix any errors | record any issues encountered
  • FRIDAY → complete final solution | submit final mission report

We are here for a limited time, and it will go by fast. By meeting our deadlines, we can insure the success of this mission. Let us get started!



Hover the images below in numerical order to receive your mission information.


ONE

Your 1st Task



You will create a website for the Mars colony. Start with a template. You will need the following four headers to contain one long page with divisions or four separate pages:

  • main section or home (index) page
  • about section or page
  • education section or page
  • contact sections or page

This will introduce the new Mars colony to planet Earth. We will promote living on the new planet, and recruit members who wish to assist in developing a thriving planet.
 TWO

Your 2nd Task



For this task, you will focus only on the home page. Be sure to include the following elements to capture the attention of those earthlings!

  • header
  • sub header
  • call to action
  • images
  • navigation
  • benefits

Let them know how great life is on Mars by describing the fun things you do and the importance of this mission.
 THREE

Your 3rd Task



For this task, you will focus only on the about page. On this page, your viewers should learn about the mission as a whole. Here are some questions to ponder while you are explaining the details of this mission:

  • Why are you here?
  • Why do you need to be here?
  • What do you need to learn?
  • What are your goals?
  • How long will you stay?
  • What have you accomplished thus far?

Remember to include supporting images!

FOUR

Your 4th Task



For this task, you will focus only on the education page. Because we are the first inhabitants on the planet, it will be important to share our new knowledge of life on Mars. Below are topics you need to discuss:

  • temperature - length of day - atmosphere
  • transportation - housing - clothing
  • food - water - exercise - entertainment
  • jobs available - current projects

I recommend setting it up like an FAQ page to share these details of Mars.
FIVE

Your 5th Task



The contact page is very important because your viewers will have many questions! Be sure to include the following in your form:

  • name entry field
  • email entry field
  • interest selection field for comments
  • comment field for questions/comments
  • level of education radio button
  • job interest checkbox for multiple choices
  • comment field to explain qualifications

You can make this into two forms if you wish, but one form can work also.
SIX

Your 6th Task



This is the final task for your mission! You will style your website to make it more appealing to your viewers. Remember to focus on the following elements:

  • color and background
  • text size and alignment
  • spacing with padding and margins
  • navigation reliability
  • smooth transitions between pages

Remember to include supporting images!
