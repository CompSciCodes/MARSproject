Hello Specialists! Thank you for reporting promptly to receive your mission assignment. We will have a series of tasks to complete in order to work toward project completion goals. The itinerary for each week is listed below:

  • MONDAY → review assignment details | record mission statement | gather with team for collaboration
  • TUESDAY → research approaches to address problem | discuss ideas and brainstorm | record ideas
  • WEDNESDAY → decide on final design | create drawing, blueprint, or flowchart of solution | start prototype
  • THURSDAY → complete prototype | test prototype and fix any errors | record any issues encountered
  • FRIDAY → complete final solution | submit final mission report

We are here for a limited time, and it will go by fast. By meeting our deadlines, we can insure the success of this mission. Let us get started!

FOUR

Your 4th Task



Mars money is in the form of a marscoin, called Mcoin, and is symbolized by a ⌾. Each ⌾ is valued at the equivalent of $1.00. Since there are no taxes on Mars, pocket change is irrelevent, so cost of items will be lowered to the nearest Mcoin amount. For example: milk cost that is $2.49 on Earth will now cost ⌾2 on Mars.

To keep the store sanitary, food will be delivered on request through a program you create. Details of this task will further instruct you on the requirements necessary for its successful completion. Be sure to include all components!

  • QR: reading - music - books - cards
  • CR: communication - extended learning

Your program should output a schedule for the week based on inputs given by individuals. Use the Mars Calendar to help you with this task and go by a 24 hour clock! See output example here.
