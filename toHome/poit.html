Report to Mission Control for Instructions


Hello Specialists! Thank you for reporting promptly to receive your mission assignment. We will have a series of tasks to complete in order to work toward project completion goals. The project layout is outlined below:

  • SECTION → Identify which Unit and Lesson is address for this task.
  • TEAM MEMBERS → Recognize the members of your team.
  • MISSION OBJECTIVE → Clearly state the goal or end result of this task.
  • PLAN → Determine what roles are assigned for each team member.
  • RESEARCH → Determine layouts, calculations, or structure types.
  • BRAINSTORMING → Generate ideas, and narrow down possibilities.
  • MODEL → Create a blueprint, flowchart, or diagram as a guide.
  • SOLUTION → Provide a link to your final executable solution.
  • PROBLEMS → List any issues that arise causing a delay in production.
  • SUMMARY → Discuss team success and what has been learned.
  • RESOURCES → Provide links to websites you used during research.

We are here for a limited time, and it will go by fast. By meeting our deadlines, we can insure the success of this mission. Let us get started!
