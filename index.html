MARS↑Project

The Future Is Now ... So Let Us Begin!

The MARS ↑ Project presents high level challenges so students can logically work through issues that impact the future of our world and beyond. Hands on activities and problem solving enhance the ability to promote out of the box thinking. Individual contribution and teamwork will be a key factor in the success of this mission. Hence its name: MARS | Meaningful Academics Relatable Solutions. ~ THE COUNTDOWN BEGINS!

You are on a mission to MARS: While on your mission, you will have many tasks based on your career field. Each of you will have the opportunity to demonstrate your new found abilities obtained through research. You will learn many skills along the way. Get ready, as it will be a busy time with many deadlines to meet!

I am excited that you are joining me on this endeavor. This mission will last approximately nine months, from September through May. Some of you are programmers, while others of you are computer IT experts. Each and every role will be an important one. Expectations will be high, as this will determine our success. Are you ready?

As a mission specialist,you will be responsible for a variety of duties to include:

  • Apply your expertise toward improving the overall quality of life on MARS.
  • Work in a team environment to insure success by all its members.
  • Commit to submitting all tasks within specified deadlines.
  • Record any problems encountered that hinder progress of a project.
  • Communicate effectively about the transition to new technologies.
  • Include technical drawings, blueprints, manuals, or flowcharts of your solutions.
  • Provide continued training and technical support to all users.
  • Maintain professionalism and ethics while in the work environment.

These expectations shall be maintained at all times until the end of this mission. I hope you are ready for the many challenges that await you. Together, we can create a sustainable and innovative community on MARS. I am looking forward to spending the year with you! Ms. Jansen
